### Our Values

#### Principled

**We are principled.** Our mission to help build a better Internet is bold and ambitious, and we take it seriously. We create our products and features with a global mindset and democratize important and innovative technologies that drive adoption of the latest standards.

#### Curious

**We are curious.** Our team is made up of pioneering innovators that approach some of the Internet’s toughest challenges with interest and a desire to learn. We are committed to providing exceptional customer service and solving customer problems in clever and technically astute ways.

#### Transparent

**We are transparent.** We don’t believe in withholding vital information from our customers, but instead prioritize clear and open communication at all times. Most importantly, we hold ourselves accountable when we make mistakes—and we do everything we can to learn from them.

The Cloudflare Capabilities outline the behaviors we value and reference when measuring success, giving feedback, and rewarding performance. They serve as the building blocks of an inclusive company culture.

PERFORMANCE = RESULTS + BEHAVIORS

When we evaluate performance, we review “what” the outcome is, **the results**. However, we also consider “how” we work and what good looks like, **the behaviors**. It doesn’t just matter what you do, but how you do it.

### Our Hiring Process

![Hiring Process](https://cf-assets.www.cloudflare.com/slt3lc6tev37/234bZCgTnXpiSBQjr1YBwe/fc33b1b72eb598b86927e1d1eb50a4bb/BDES_4945_Update_Visual_Elements_for_Careers_Site_hiring_process_alpha_r01_1.png)

#### Meeting the Team

We've designed our hiring process so that you have the opportunity to meet a variety of Cloudflare team members that you will work directly with: your prospective manager, peers from the team, and key stakeholders.

These conversations will help us understand how you think, what you’re passionate about, as well as which skills and creative problem-solving abilities you bring to the table. We also want you to get to know us too — our interviewers will be ready to answer all of your questions!

#### Orange Cloud Interview

During the hiring process you’ll have an Orange Cloud Interview which will focus on the Cloudflare Capabilities and the behaviors that we value. The interviewer will ask you to share examples of how you do your work and make an impact, rather than assessing your role-specific skills, knowledge, or experience.

So, how do you prepare for this interview? Consider how you display these values in your everyday work, reflect on recent situations at work, whether you’ve learnt from these experiences, and if you’d do anything differently if they were to happen again.
